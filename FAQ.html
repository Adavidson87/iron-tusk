We recommend that you keep the bandage on, covering your tattoo for the first night. It can be removed the next day when showering.

When you wash your tattoo, wash with soap and warm water then pat dry with a clean paper towel or let it air dry. Afterwards apply a thin layer of ointment that is meant for tattoo aftercare. We can provide some to you or at some suggestions. Your tattoo will need to breath while it is healing so do not use an excessive amount of ointment on it. The ointment will need to be applied several times a day, every two to three hours.

It is advisable to keep your tattoo covered while you sleep for the first few days to keep it from rubbing against or sticking to clothes or bedsheets.

The tattoo should begin to peel, much like a sunburn, after a few days of proper care. Do not pick at it while it does, let it come off naturally.

You should keep your new tattoo covered when out in the sun for the first few weeks while it is still healing, and you should avoid swimming or any activity that involves soaking your tattoo in water. That means avoiding swimming pools, lakes, rivers, oceans, hot tubs, etc.

It is normal for your tattoo to itch. Please do not scratch it when it does as it can cause damage to your tattoo. Instead lightly pat it or apply lotion.
