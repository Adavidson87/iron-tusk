Welcome to Iron Tusk Tattoos!

We are an artist owned and operated shop located in downtown McMinnville, TN.

Shop hours are 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM Tuesday through Saturday.

Walk-ins are always welcome!

EVENTS

We are going to hold our inaugural inner shop tattoo competition! The competition will be held Saturday, July 9th at 11:30am. Each Iron Tusk tattoo artist will come up with an original full color Neo-Traditional tattoo design (designs will be released by May 21st ). We are asking for volunteers to be canvases. If you are selected, your tattoo will be FREE! Lunch will be provided, and you will have an original tattoo design on your body!

To qualify you must:

  1. Like and share this post on Facebook
  2. Come into the shop to submit your name and contact info by July 1st
  3. Be able to sit for a tattoo for 6 - 8 hours
  4. Have a great attitude and be ready for fun!

How the competition will work

The people who will be canvases will be selected at random on July 2nd. The 4 people selected will choose their tattoo artist at random and get the design the artist came up with. We will post the final tattoo without identifying the artist. We will leave voting open for 3 days. Whoever wins will get a trophy and bragging rights.
